# Social Media Checklist for Responsible Use

## ✅ Do's
- Think before posting any content online
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone
- Verify information before sharing
- Protect your personal information and privacy
- Use strong passwords and enable security settings

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share sensitive personal details publicly
- Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information
- Do not post inappropriate or offensive content
- Avoid oversharing your daily activities or location
- Do not engage in online arguments or negativity
