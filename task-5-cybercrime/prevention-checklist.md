# Stay Safe Online – Checklist  
*(For College Students in India)*

- Use strong, unique passwords for every account (avoid using your name or birthdate).

- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.

- Never click on suspicious links or download attachments from unknown emails or messages.

- Always verify website URLs before logging in (look for `https://` and correct spelling).

- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts like banking or email.

- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank.

- **Remember:** You don’t need to enter a UPI PIN to receive money—only to send it.

- Log out from shared or public computers after use.

- Regularly back up important data to avoid loss from ransomware attacks.

- Report cybercrime:
  - Website: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
  - Helpline: 1930
